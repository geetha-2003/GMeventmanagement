wedding Planning

The overall horizon of the wedding planner in simple words is to plan the entire wedding. The idea is to make sure that right from picking the venue to managing your wedding entry and guests along with the finances and other miscellaneous things, there is a professional helping hand who does not make it as overwhelming as it seems from a bottom-up view.


           Whether you are willing to jump into us in your last month of preparation for your “big day” or just weeks before it, Enlightening Events makes sure everything goes off smoothly. Be it for either full service or just for coordination, we meet with the clients and get their full objective as to what exactly they want on their big day. And if they are confused as to what they need to treat their guests with, leave everything with us. Lean back, enjoy the big day without any hustle and bustle.
